Visiting Instructions

  • Entry in anytime on open days is possible, but booking is recommended.
  • Do not take any food or water bottles inside the galleries.
  • Children under 3 are not allowed in certain exhibits.
  • Photography without flash is permitted, but video is not.
  • Please do not touch the artifacts.
  • Bags may be subject to search upon entry.
